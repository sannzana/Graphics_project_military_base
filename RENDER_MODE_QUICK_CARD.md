## ?? RENDER MODE COMPARISON

### Truck K (Render Mode)
- Color Only: Solid green (0.43, 0.46, 0.33)
- Texture: truck.png details
- Blended: 50% color + 50% texture

### Ground M (Render Mode)
- Color Only: Solid sand (0.8, 0.8, 0.7)
- Texture: ff.png 4x tiled
- Blended: 50% color + 50% texture

### Jeep (NO Render Mode)
- Always: jeep.png texture applied
- No buttons to change
- Always looks the same
