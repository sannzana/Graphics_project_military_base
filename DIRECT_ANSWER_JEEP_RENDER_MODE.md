# ? DIRECT ANSWER: JEEP RENDER MODE BUTTONS

## ?? YOUR QUESTION
**"What are the buttons for jeep for changing blended, texture, etc?"**

---

## ? ANSWER

### **JEEPS DO NOT HAVE RENDER MODE BUTTONS**

Jeeps **ALWAYS display with texture** (jeep.png)  
There is **NO button to change** to:
- ? Color-only mode
- ? Blended mode (color + texture)
- ? Any other render variation

---

## ?? WHAT DOES HAVE RENDER MODES

### ? TRUCK - Button: **K**
Press K to cycle:
1. **Color Only** (solid green)
2. **Texture** (detailed truck.png)
3. **Blended** (50% color + 50% texture)
4. Back to Color (cycle repeats)

### ? GROUND - Button: **M**
Press M to cycle:
1. **Color Only** (solid sand)
2. **Texture** (ff.png 4x tiled)
3. **Blended** (50% color + 50% texture)
4. Back to Color (cycle repeats)

### ? JEEP - No Buttons
- Always textured
- Cannot change appearance
- No render mode options

---
